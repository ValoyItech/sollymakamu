About G-One

From the heart of Limpopo emerges a true renaissance artist, bringing the rich culture of Xitsonga to the world stage

Solly Makamu

Known professionally as G-One (Makhense)

Mdabula Village, Malamulele
Limpopo, South Africa
Xitsonga Cultural Ambassador

Multi-Talented Entertainer

G-One represents the epitome of artistic versatility, seamlessly blending traditional Xitsonga culture with contemporary entertainment. His journey from Mdabula village to becoming a recognized icon showcases the power of passion, dedication, and cultural pride.

As both G-One and Makhense, Solly has created a unique identity that resonates with audiences across multiple platforms and mediums, making him a true ambassador of Tsonga culture in the modern entertainment landscape.
